About Superrr

Superrr develops visions and projects with the goal to create more equitable futures. We do so by researching technologies, building networks and shaping new narratives. Superrr is playful, visionary and feminist.

Superrr is a non-profit that was founded by Elisa Lindinger and Julia Kloiber. We work on an international level with non-profits, companies and public institutions alike. Our clients and partners included the Mozilla Foundation, the Ford Foundation, Stiftung Neue Verantwortung, the Helmholtz Association, Google and others. We advised the commision of inquiry on artificial intelligence of the German parliament, and the European Commission. We have presented at national and international conferences and events, such as re-publica, TED, Public Democracy Forum, the School of Disobedience and more.
